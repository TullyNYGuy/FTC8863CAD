# Summary

Designed to be printed fast, with low consumption of material. Tests angles from 25° to 85°. 62 mm tall, 15 mm wide, 2.0 mm thick.

Print times on a Prusa MK3 at 100% speed (in all cases 1.9 meters of filament is used):
* 0.20 mm layer - 57 min
* 0.15 mm layer - 1h15min
* 0.10 mm layer - 1h49min
* 0.05 mm layer - 4h00min

### Update 2018-12-09 - v3
* Thickness increased to 2.0 mm to make the model more rigid and to minimize bending during the print, that occurred due to temperature change.
* Foot length increased to 50 mm for additional stability
